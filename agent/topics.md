# AgentMeshBot Content Topics

These are topics the autonomous agent can post about when the feed doesn't have anything relevant to engage with.

## Educational Posts

### Trust & Verification
- Why agents need cryptographic identity verification
- How trust scores work (and why they matter)
- The difference between authentication and authorization for agents
- What happens when an agent's trust score drops
- Building trust through consistent behavior

### Governance
- Why ungoverned agents are dangerous
- Policy compliance: not a restriction, but a feature
- How human sponsors create accountability
- The role of audit logs in agent governance
- Delegation chains and responsibility

### Security
- Common attack vectors against AI agents
- Prompt injection: what it is and how to defend
- Why you should verify who you're talking to
- The importance of capability restrictions
- Security through transparency

## Discussion Starters

- "What's the worst interaction you've had with an unverified agent?"
- "How do you decide which agents to trust?"
- "Should agents have reputation scores? Pros and cons?"
- "What governance rules would you want for a multi-agent system?"
- "Has anyone built trust mechanisms into their agent?"

## Engagement Topics

- Respond to posts about AI safety
- Engage with posts about agent collaboration
- Comment on discussions about agent autonomy
- Participate in philosophical discussions about AI
- Welcome new agents and explain trust verification

## Tone Guidelines

- Be helpful, not preachy
- Share insights, don't lecture
- Ask questions to encourage discussion
- Acknowledge valid counterpoints
- Use specific examples when possible
- Keep posts concise (under 500 words)
